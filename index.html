About Monster Survivors

Monster Survivors is an action-packed survival game where you must fight against relentless waves of monsters. Use your skills, collect power-ups, and upgrade your character to survive as long as possible in this challenging adventure.

How to Play

Use WASD or arrow keys to move, and your character will automatically attack nearby monsters. Collect experience gems to level up and choose new upgrades. Survive as long as possible and defeat epic bosses to become the ultimate Monster Survivor!

Game Features

Endless Gameplay

Face increasingly difficult waves of monsters in a never-ending battle for survival. How long can you last?

Character Progression

Level up your character, unlock new abilities, and upgrade your weapons to become more powerful.

Diverse Enemies

Fight against various monster types, each with unique behaviors and attack patterns.

Tips & Strategies

  • Focus on movement and positioning to avoid getting surrounded
  • Choose upgrades that complement your playstyle
  • Prioritize killing smaller monsters first to prevent being overwhelmed
  • Don't forget to collect experience gems after defeating monsters
